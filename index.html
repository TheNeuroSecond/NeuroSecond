NeuroSecond is at the forefront of neural interface technology, dedicated to creating revolutionary solutions that bridge the gap between the human brain and digital systems.

Founded in 2025 by Nadim, NeuroSecond brings together cutting-edge neuroscience research with advanced engineering to develop systems that interpret brainwave patterns with unprecedented accuracy.

Our mission is to create accessible neural interface technology that enhances human capabilities and opens new possibilities for communication, rehabilitation, and cognitive enhancement.

Based in Dhaka, Bangladesh, our team is working to transform how humans interact with technology through non-invasive brain-computer interfaces.
